About Scott

Scott has been organizing for over 30 years. Starting at 6 years old when his mom took him to protest a proposed trash incinerator near there home. He started professionally at 18 as a paid petitioner and spent years canvassing and organizing around Ohio before becoming a Canvass Director at Fieldworks and later a National Field Director at Community Outreach Group (now Scale to Change), leading programs with over 10 million combined knocks at Planned Parenthood. In 2020, he switched from field to digital, running the Sierra Club's Digital Distributed organizing program during the pandemic, helping thousands of volunteers write letters, make calls, and send texts.

In 2021, he took a data job at For Our Future to learn more about the data and systems behind the scenes. After the 2022 midterms, he went back to school to fill in knowledge gaps, learning Python, SQL, and web development through a data science program at LA City College. Today, he's a Deputy Director of Engineering and Analytics at NextGen America, working on targeting, analytics, and data pipelines. Outside of work, he's got a partner of 11 years, two Shiba Inus, loves hot yoga, Lagree, and video games. 🎮
